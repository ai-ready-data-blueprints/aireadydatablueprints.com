Engagement Process

How to Request

  1. Initial Contact: Reach out via LinkedIn or contact page
  2. Requirements Discussion: 30-minute discovery call
  3. Proposal: Detailed scope, timeline, and pricing
  4. Agreement: Contract execution and scheduling
  5. Delivery: Engagement execution and follow-up

What to Include

When reaching out, please provide:

  • Organization name and industry
  • Engagement type and objectives
  • Preferred timeline and duration
  • Team size and technical background
  • Budget range (if applicable)
  • Specific topics or challenges
